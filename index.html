Interactive Demo 02

Emissions, CO2, and Arctic Sea Ice

Here emissions E build atmospheric CO2 via dC/dt = E - k(C - C0). A normalized CO2 level then acts like the forcing parameter in a cubic ice-loss model, while the ice animation shows annual growth and melt around a shrinking mean state.
